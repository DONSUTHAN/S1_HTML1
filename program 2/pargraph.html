engineering collage of vadakara

College of Engineering Vadakara (CEV), located in Vadakara, Kerala, is a prominent government engineering institution established in 1999 under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE), an autonomous society under the Government of Kerala. Affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by AICTE, CEV offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines such as Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Electrical and Electronics, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering. The college is known for its strong academic environment, well-equipped laboratories, experienced faculty, and active student community. It also emphasizes research, innovation, and entrepreneurship through various technical clubs, industry tie-ups, and incubation programs. With a scenic campus, modern infrastructure, and a focus on holistic development, College of Engineering Vadakara stands out as a hub for quality technical education in northern Kerala.
